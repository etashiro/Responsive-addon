Why Kenshi Yonezu?

Why is Kenshi Yonezu the best?

His historic Billboard chart achievement with "Lemon," as the first-ever act to claim the song of the year for two consecutive years, underscores his remarkable talent and enduring appeal. Beyond this, Yonezu's music is a window into his unique perspective, where he weaves beautiful melodies with poetic lyrics, inviting listeners to connect with his emotions and experiences. What sets him apart even further is his hands-on approach to his artistry. He not only creates captivating music but also actively contributes to his visual identity by personally illustrating his music videos and album covers, ensuring a cohesive and immersive artistic experience. His dedication to crafting a complete and authentic connection with his audience, coupled with his exceptional musical prowess, solidifies Yonezu Kenshi's status as a truly great artist.
